Welcome to Zooland!


At Zooland, we offer a unique and captivating experience for animal enthusiasts of all ages. Our zoo is home to a diverse range of fascinating creatures from around the world. Located in a lush and expansive natural setting, Zooland provides a safe and comfortable environment for our animal residents.


As you explore our zoo, you'll have the opportunity to encounter majestic lions, graceful giraffes, playful monkeys, and many other captivating species. We believe in promoting conservation and education, and our knowledgeable staff is always ready to share interesting facts about the animals and their habitats.


At Zooland, we prioritize the well-being and care of our animals. Our dedicated team of experts ensures that they receive the highest quality of care, proper nutrition, and a stimulating environment that closely resembles their natural habitats. We are committed to fostering a deep understanding and appreciation for wildlife conservation.

Goals of Zooland:


  1. Conservation: Zooland is committed to the conservation of wildlife and their habitats. Our primary goal is to contribute to the preservation and protection of endangered species through breeding programs, research initiatives, and active participation in conservation projects.
  2. Education: We aim to educate and raise awareness among visitors about the importance of wildlife conservation. Through educational programs, we strive to inspire a sense of wonder and appreciation for the natural world.

    3. "Nature is not a place to visit. It is home." - Gary Snyder

  3. Research: Zooland actively supports scientific research efforts related to animal behavior, ecology, and conservation. By collaborating with researchers and institutions, we contribute to expanding knowledge and understanding of various species, their habitats, and the challenges they face.
  4. Animal Welfare: We prioritize the health, well-being, and enrichment of our animal residents. Zooland adheres to the highest standards of animal care, providing spacious and stimulating habitats that meet their physical and behavioral needs.
  5. Breeding and Species Survival: Zooland participates in breeding programs aimed at ensuring the survival of endangered species. By maintaining genetically diverse populations, we contribute to the long-term viability of these species.
